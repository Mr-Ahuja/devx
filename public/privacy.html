Privacy Policy

Effective:

This policy covers the DevX web application and the accompanying browser extension (the “Extension”). DevX provides client‑side developer utilities (e.g., JSON/XML formatting and a Diff tool).

In short: DevX does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Data Collection

  • No account, login, or personal identifiers are required.
  • No analytics, tracking pixels, or cookies are used.
  • No data you enter into the tools is sent to our servers; processing is performed entirely client‑side.

Extension Permissions

The current Extension version (0.1.0) does not request any optional or host permissions. It only provides a popup UI.

If a future version requires additional permissions, this page will list what changed and why in the version history below.

Processing & Storage

  • Inputs you paste or type are processed in memory within your browser.
  • Nothing is uploaded to external services unless you explicitly choose to share content elsewhere.
  • Some features may use URL parameters (e.g., base64 text for Diff deep‑links). Be mindful that sharing such links shares the encoded content in the URL.
  • No persistent storage is used by the Extension for your content. If a future version stores preferences, they will reside only in your browser (e.g., local storage).

Third‑Party Sharing

DevX does not share any data with third parties. There are no embedded third‑party trackers.

Your Choices

  • Use the tools without logging in or identifying yourself.
  • Avoid sharing deep‑link URLs if they contain sensitive content in query parameters.
  • Close the page or clear the URL to remove any embedded content in the address bar.

Security

DevX aims to minimize risk by operating entirely client‑side and requesting minimal permissions. As with any software, avoid pasting sensitive data on shared or untrusted devices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when functionality or permissions change. Material changes will be reflected in the version history below.

Version History (Privacy‑Impacting Changes)

  • 0.1.0 – Initial release of the Extension. No data collection, no analytics, no host permissions.
